C2C in action

How it works.

Getting high-resolution video and photos directly from the camera to Frame.io is surprisingly easy, with perks that go way beyond speed.

Simple secure setup

Pair your Camera to Cloud device.

Securely link your C2C-enabled device to your Frame.io account with just a few clicks. Then in the Frame.io app, set up permissions with only a few taps. Your C2C device connects with write-only access to your Frame.io account, and you control who can view or access anything.

Automatic uploads and file structure

Get proxies on the spot.

C2C uploads high-quality proxy files in formats such as H.264, H.265, or ProRes. You can edit these proxies immediately, then relink to the original camera media later with just a few clicks.

More than just video files.

C2C-enabled devices allow for automatic uploads of photos, audio, camera logs, or Color Decision Lists (CDLs). And all files are intuitively organized in folders by date, type, and device.

Instant access anywhere

View on any device.

Reviewers anywhere in the world can use our apps — or their browser — to view the latest takes, right from the set.

Postproduction can start immediately.

Media is uploaded directly to Frame.io so editors can pull assets into their creative tools using integrations with many major editing apps.

Convenience

Attend the shoot without the travel.

Make the latest takes instantly available to your whole team.

Avoid pickups and reshoots.

Review takes and make key adjustments while you’re in production to prevent costly reshoots later.

Make different versions faster.

Quickly create cuts for various audiences or countries. Editing can start almost in real time.

Keep track of multiple shoots.

Monitor simultaneous shoots in different locations. Video village is now only a click away.

Engage fans in the moment.

Choose takes from live events in progress, and then post to social media before it’s old news.
