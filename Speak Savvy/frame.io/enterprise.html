Static Watermarking is a basic text-based watermark that is constant — remaining the same across all projects and all playback sessions within a team.

Session-based Watermarking (Watermark ID) visually overlay real-time information based on the specific session; like user, date, IP, etc.

Forensic Watermarking is an invisible watermark that is embedded at the pixel level — per session and on the fly — enabling forensic investigation to the identify the source of the original stream.
