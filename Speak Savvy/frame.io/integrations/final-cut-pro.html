Power to

Integration

One workspace to
rule them all.

Frame.io lets you share cuts, view stakeholder feedback, and manage the entire project — all without ever leaving Final Cut Pro. This is next-level speed and efficiency for your entire post-production team.

Collaboration

Consolidate and conquer.

Achieve faster delivery times by streamlining how your teams share, collect, and address feedback. And it all happens inside Final Cut Pro.

Centralize files.

Share and manage assets, footage, and entire projects with collaborators — all inside Final Cut Pro. Frame.io is your central platform for all of it, including Camera to Cloud footage right from the set.

Export directly to Frame.io.

Export sequences directly to Frame.io from Final Cut Pro with a single click. Stakeholders have instant access, and they can review and leave feedback on any device using their browser or the Frame.io app.

Simplify communication and feedback.

Respond to comment threads and address timecode-accurate notes — all without ever leaving your editing space.
