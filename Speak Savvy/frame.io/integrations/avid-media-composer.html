Integration

Stay in the zone.

Frame.io lets you share files and media, collect feedback, and manage entire projects faster than ever before.

Collaboration

Consolidate and conquer.

Here are a few of the ways Frame.io helps to streamline and accelerate your Avid workflow.

Centralize files.

Share and manage assets, footage, and entire projects using the Frame.io Transfer App. Frame.io is your central platform for everything, including Camera to Cloud dailies with matching timecode, right from the set.

Export and share easily to Frame.io.

Export your timeline to a Watch Folder using the Frame.io Mac app, or in Windows simply drag and drop to the Transfer App. Stakeholders are notified immediately and can view and comment on any device.

Simplify communication and feedback.

Download reviewer comments as an Avid XML file to view them as timecode-accurate markers in your timeline — then jump from one marker to the next to address each note. Less mind-reading, more delivering.
